Cherry Jackpot Game

Click once on the 'start Spin' button to start the spinning of the numbers. after 15 Seconds you will be left with 3 numbers in 3 different boxes. If these 3 numbers happen to be equal, your score increases by 1 unit else it decreases by 1 unit. During the spin, you can click on the 'stop spin' to stop the spin in the middle with some 3 numbers.
